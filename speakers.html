    ADIL RASHID

    Musician, composer and producer. Associated with Underground Authority(founder), Euphoria, and Paloma and Adil as well as an independent composer and producer. I have prepared a very relatable analogy about music, with how people converse and how different opinions and perspectives actually come together to contribute to a discussion. Just like in music, the basic building blocks, be it notes or chords or rhythms, can all be compared to speech and expression and makes a more comprehensive understanding of the “language of music”, which is the title of the talk. It fuses the academic approach to music and tried to explain it in context to everyday life. Hoping to make the idea of making music more approachable, and relatable.

    ALAKANANDA ROY

    The name Ms. Alokananda Roy, conjures an image of grace and elegance. Her versatility comes through in her innovative dancing styles and creative stage productions. Taking great pleasure, in being involved with varied creative activities, did never refrain her from extending genuine care and compassion to the society at large. She has always been unprejudiced and non-judgemental. That perhaps, initiated her to adopt the secluded world of convicted individuals in the Correctional Homes of West Bengal, as her arena and work for their welfare, through Dance and Music. Her organization Touch World operates to care for the children of the convicts, deprived of family support. The reward she treasures however is the unconditional love of the millions that she has received and is still receiving. Her sole expectation from the society is understanding, compassion, forgiveness and empathy for the less fortunate.

Ashish Vidyarthi is also a traveller and a motivational speaker. In his journey over the last 24 years, he has had amazing experiences across varied cultures and geographies that have helped him discover inner resources to celebrate the ‘journey’ itself.

    IROM SHARMILA

    Irom Chanu Sharmila (born 14 March 1972),also known as the "Iron Lady"or "Mengoubi" ("the fair one") is a civil rights activist, political activist, and poet from the Indian state of Manipur. On 5 November 2000,she began a hunger strike which she ended on 9 August 2016, after 16 years of fasting. Having refused food and water for more than 500 weeks, she has been called "the world's longest hunger striker".On International Women’s Day, 2014 she was voted the top woman icon of India by MSN Poll.
